About

Musical, practical and personal bass tuition.

Hooked On Bass UK is built around helping students develop real musical confidence — combining strong technique, practical theory, groove, creativity and enjoyment.

Teaching Approach

Structured lessons without losing the joy of playing.

Lessons are shaped around each student’s goals, musical taste and current ability. Whether you are completely new to the bass or already playing in bands, the aim is to build useful skills that transfer directly into real music.

Sessions can cover technique, rhythm, theory, songs, improvisation, tone, performance preparation and creative musicianship — always with a clear sense of direction.

Values

What lessons are built around

Clarity

Clear explanations, focused goals and practical exercises that make sense.

Creativity

Lessons that encourage musical personality, expression and confidence.

Progress

Step-by-step development that helps students hear and feel improvement.
