About

Hi, I’m Joe.

Music has always been a huge part of my life, and Hooked On Bass is my way of sharing the parts of bass playing that still get me excited.

Hooked On Bass

Friendly, practical bass tuition built around the music you enjoy.

My aim is to make bass playing feel accessible, enjoyable and easy to understand — especially for beginners and intermediate players who want to build solid foundations.

Lessons are shaped around your development, your musical taste and the kind of player you want to become. We can work through technique, timing, songs, theory, groove, tone, live playing and how the bass functions in a band setting.

I like to combine traditional bass playing approaches with extended techniques and creative ideas, helping students develop confidence while also finding their own voice on the instrument.

Approach

Learn the instrument, understand the role, enjoy the process.

Bass is a brilliant instrument because it sits right at the centre of rhythm, harmony and feel. A good bassline can completely shape how a song moves.

In lessons, I try to help students understand not just what to play, but why it works — whether that means locking in with drums, supporting a song, building a groove or creating something more personal.

I’m also a member of the Musicians’ Union.
