Lessons

What to include in a lesson enquiry.

If you are getting in touch about lessons, it helps to include your playing level, the kind of music you enjoy, where you are based, and whether you are interested in 30, 45 or 60 minute lessons.

New students usually start with two 30-minute introductory sessions, which gives us a chance to get to know each other before settling into regular lessons.

Lessons are currently taught in person across Derby and Nottingham.

Servicing

What to include in a servicing enquiry.

If you are enquiring about bass servicing, please include the make and model of the instrument, what you would like done, and whether you would like new strings fitted.

If new strings are needed, please provide the strings you would like fitted.

The standard setup and clean is £55 and includes collection and return delivery within Derby. A surcharge may apply outside Derby.
