Terms

Lesson terms and conditions.

Clear expectations help lessons run smoothly and keep things fair for students, parents and tutor.

1. Lesson Bookings

Lessons are arranged directly between Hooked On Bass and the student, or parent/guardian where appropriate. Lesson times are subject to availability and are confirmed once both parties have agreed a suitable date, time and lesson length.

2. Introductory Sessions

New students usually begin with a buy one, get one free introduction offer: two 30-minute sessions designed to help both student and tutor get to know each other. These sessions help establish musical interests, current ability, goals and the best direction for future lessons.

3. Lesson Format

Lessons are currently taught in person across Derby and Nottingham. Lessons usually take place at the student's home. A chair and access to a plug socket are required.

4. Payment

Payment can be made by cash or bank transfer. Lesson prices are listed on the pricing page and may be updated from time to time. Any travel surcharge will be discussed before lessons are agreed.

5. Travel

The first 4 miles of travel from the tutor's location are included. After this, a surcharge of £1 per mile may apply. Travel arrangements and any additional cost will be confirmed before lessons begin.

6. Cancellations

Lessons must be cancelled or rearranged with at least 48 hours notice. Lessons cancelled with less than 48 hours notice are still chargeable, except in exceptional circumstances.

7. Lateness

If a student is late, the lesson will usually still finish at the agreed time. If the tutor is delayed, the missed time will be made up or adjusted appropriately.

8. Lesson Agreement

Once lessons are established and both sides are comfortable, usually around the one to two month mark, students or parents/guardians may be asked to sign a simple lesson agreement covering payment, cancellations, expectations and policies.

9. Student Resources

Any PDFs, lesson notes, exercises, backing tracks or other resources provided by Hooked On Bass are for personal student use only and should not be redistributed without permission.

10. Safeguarding

Hooked On Bass holds an Enhanced DBS check for working with children and keeps up to date with regular safeguarding training. For younger students, lessons should be arranged with clear communication between tutor and parent/guardian.

11. Professional Membership

Hooked On Bass is a member of the Musicians’ Union and aims to provide a reliable, professional and respectful service for students and families.

12. Privacy and Data

Any personal information provided through enquiries or lessons is handled in accordance with GDPR principles. Information is only stored where necessary, kept accurate and up to date where possible, and is never sold or shared with third parties unless required by law or necessary for safeguarding purposes.

13. Changes to Terms

These terms may be updated from time to time. The most recent version will be available on this page.
