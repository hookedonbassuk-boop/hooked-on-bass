Terms

Lesson terms and conditions.

These terms are a working draft and can be adjusted before the website goes live.

1. Lesson Bookings

Lessons are arranged directly between the tutor and student, or parent/guardian where appropriate. Lesson times are subject to availability and are confirmed once both parties have agreed a date and time.

2. Payment

Payment terms will be confirmed before lessons begin. Lessons may be paid individually or as part of a block booking where offered.

3. Cancellations

If a student needs to cancel or rearrange a lesson, as much notice as possible should be given. A minimum cancellation notice period may apply and should be confirmed before booking.

4. Missed Lessons

Missed lessons may still be charged if insufficient notice is given, except in exceptional circumstances.

5. Lateness

If a student arrives late, the lesson will usually still finish at the agreed time. If the tutor is delayed, the missed time will be made up or adjusted appropriately.

6. Online Lessons

Students are responsible for having a suitable internet connection, device, audio setup and instrument for online lessons.

7. Student Resources

PDFs, lesson notes, backing tracks or other resources provided by Hooked On Bass UK are for personal student use only and should not be redistributed without permission.

8. Safeguarding

For younger students, lessons should be arranged with clear communication between the tutor and parent or guardian. Any specific safeguarding arrangements can be discussed before lessons begin.

9. Changes to Terms

These terms may be updated from time to time. The most recent version will be available on this page.
