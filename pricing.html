Intro Offer

Start with two 30-minute sessions.

New students usually begin with a buy one, get one free introduction: two 30-minute sessions designed to help us get to know each other.

These first lessons help establish your playing level, musical interests, goals and the best direction for future development.

They also give you a chance to see how I teach before settling into regular lessons.

Travel

Lessons at the student’s home.

Lessons are currently taught in person at the student’s home across Derby and Nottingham. I only need a chair and access to a plug socket.

The first 4 miles of travel from my location are included free of charge. After that, an additional £1 per mile is added to help cover travel costs.

Any travel surcharge will be discussed before lessons begin.
