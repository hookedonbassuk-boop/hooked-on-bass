Bass Servicing

Give your bass the TLC it deserves.

Setup, cleaning and care for basses that need a little love — helping your instrument feel better, play better and stay reliable.

Setup & Clean

£55 bass setup and cleaning service.

My servicing work is aimed at players who want their bass to feel comfortable, clean and ready to play. Sometimes an instrument does not need anything dramatic — just proper attention, adjustment and care.

The service is £55 and includes collection and delivery back within Derby. If you are outside Derby, a travel surcharge may apply and will be agreed before collection.

I usually ask for the instrument for at least one week so there is enough time to check it properly, carry out the work carefully and arrange return delivery.

What’s Included

Care, adjustment and attention to detail.

Setup Check

General playability check, action assessment, intonation check and practical adjustment where needed.

Cleaning

Instrument body, fingerboard and fret cleaning to help your bass feel fresh and cared for.

Restringing

If new strings are required, customers should provide their preferred set of strings.

Collection & Return

Simple collection and delivery.

Instruments are collected and returned by Hooked On Bass unless otherwise agreed. Please make sure the bass is supplied in a suitable hard or soft case for safe transport.

Collection and delivery within Derby are included in the £55 price. If you are outside Derby, I will confirm any travel surcharge before arranging the work.

Once the work is complete, I will contact you to arrange a suitable return time.

Before Booking

A few useful things to know.

Turnaround

Please allow at least one week for the service, depending on availability and the condition of the instrument.

Strings

New strings are not included in the service price. If you would like a restring, please provide the strings you want fitted.

Extra Work

If additional parts or repair work are needed, this will be discussed and agreed before continuing.
