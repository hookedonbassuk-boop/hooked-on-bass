Lessons

Bass lessons that grow with you.

Friendly one-to-one tuition for beginners and intermediate players, shaped around your musical taste, development and long-term progress.

How Lessons Begin

Start with two 30-minute sessions.

New students begin with a buy one, get one free introduction: two 30-minute sessions designed to help us get to know each other.

These first lessons are a relaxed way for me to understand your playing level, your musical taste, how you learn, and what you would like to get from the bass. They also give you a chance to see how I teach before committing longer-term.

From there, we can build a lesson plan that feels useful, realistic and personal.

Practical skills for real playing.

Long-Term Progress

Good musicianship takes consistency.

Bass playing is not something you “complete” in a few weeks. Like any musical skill, real progress comes from regular playing, curiosity and steady development over time.

Short bursts can be useful, but the students who improve the most are usually the ones who keep showing up, keep practising and allow the work to build gradually.

My aim is to help you build habits, confidence and understanding that last — not just rush through a few songs and leave you stuck later.
